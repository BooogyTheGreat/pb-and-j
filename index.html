Peanut Butter and Jelly

Lets learn how to make a Peanut butter jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Grape Jelly
  • 2 Slices of Bread

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of Bread
  2. Spread the Peanut Butter on one slice
  3. Spread your jelly on the other slice
  4. Carefully lift the slice with peanut butter
  5. Slowly lower it Peanut butter side down
  6. Make contact square in the middle of the bread

How to EAT

  1. Pick up the Sandwich
  2. Open your mouth
  3. Put the sandwich in the mouth
  4. Close your Mouth
  5. EAT
